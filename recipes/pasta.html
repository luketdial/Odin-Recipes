You need a Mezzaluna

This is a amazing meal inspire by the anime "Food Wars". Together we can create this dish in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken breast cut into chunks
  • 4 tbsp Curry powder
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  • 8 oz Fettuccine or linguine pasta
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp Curry
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • Topping Parmesan cheese & Fresh parsley

Now lets get started with the creation of this deliciuos meal.

For the Chicken

  1. In a medium pot filled with water, add the oil, salt and the pasta.
  2. Cook the pasta until al dente, usually takes up to 7-10 minutes.
  3. Once it has done cooking, drain and set aside.

Cooking the Chicken

  1. In a bowl, add the chunks of chunks along with the seasonings and Worcestershire sauce. Mix everything together and set aside.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, heat the oil then add the chicken.
  3. Cook the chicken for about 10-12 minutes or until the chicken is golden brown.

Making the Sauce

  1. When the chicken has done cooking, stir in the heavy cream, curry powder, salt and pepper.
  2. Let it simmer for 3-5 minutes before adding the pasta to the sauce. Mix everything together and serve.
